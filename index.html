About Us

Casa Latina is comprised of two ethos of handicrafts, one focusing primarily on Panamanian crafts stemming from different indigenous groups primarily from the Darien region. The second focused on the Andean region of Latin America. We invite you to experience the beauty and historical importance of Latin American folk art.
